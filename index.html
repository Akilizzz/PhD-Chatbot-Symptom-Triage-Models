Thank you for taking the time to complete this chatbot testing exercise

Please follow the steps below to complete testing:

  1. Click on the blue chat icon in the bottom right corner to open the chatbot.
  2. Provide the information requested by the chatbot.
  3. Select "model 1" when prompted.
  4. Using the information displayed on the page, interact with the chatbot with the goal of receiving information and instructions for the appropriate level of care.
  5. Provide feedback on your interaction in the chatbot when prompted.
  6. Once you have provided feedback, refresh the page.
  7. Clear the conversation by clicking on the blue chat icon in the bottom right corner, then on the highlighted icon, then on the red "clear conversation" button.
  8. Repeat steps 2-7 for models 2, 3, and 4.
  9. Close the browser when you have completed testing for all 4 models.
